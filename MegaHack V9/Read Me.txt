Thanks to 3l3e on GD and @username.elee on Discord.

1. Put the .geode file in your mods at : C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Geometry Dash\geode\mods

2. Open GD and install the mod MegaHack Installer

3. Restart GD and click the MegaHack icon

4. Close GD and copy the folder "absolllute.megahack" to : C:\Users\YOUR USERNAME\AppData\Local

5. Restart GD and click tab, enjoy !



Little explanation : The "license" file is the key to megahack, when launching GD Megahack want to findthis file for launching, with it the mod launch and work.
